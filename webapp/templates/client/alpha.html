Geopoly

Welcome to the Geopoly alpha testing! First, read this quick tutorial about the rules and hotkeys:

Rules

  • Countries Each player controls 1 country.
  • Turns The game consits of turns.
  • Armies Each player starts win an army. An army consists of several units. Each unit type has different abilities and stats.
  • Moving By clicking on one of your areas you can move your units to a neighboring area.
  • Splitting armies You can move each of your armies once per turn. Splitting your armies is allowed.
  • Merging armies Merging one of your armies into another one will exhaust both armies, meaning that you can't move either of them anymore during that turn.
  • Battle Moving your army onto an enemy army will result in a battle.
  • Winning a battle If the attacker wins the battle, the remaining part of that army will conquer the defeated area.
  • Losing a battle If the lost defender has some army left, they will be forced to a friendly neighbor area
  • Annihilation by encirclement If the defender can't move to any neighboring area, its remaining army will be annihilated.
  • Winning a battle as defender If the defender wins the battle, however, the attacker moves its army back to its original area.
  • Winning the game The aim of the game is to destroy your enemies!

Hotkeys and GUI

  • Q (+ click if smartcasting is off): Opens area dialog which lists units on that area.
  • click on right corner: ends your current turn
  • SPACE or click on left corner: will jump to one of your areas
  • click on area: if this is your area, you can click on a neighboring area to move your army there
